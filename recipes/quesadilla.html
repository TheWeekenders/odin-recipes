Quesadilla Recipe

Gooey GOODNESS!

Let's take a deeper dive into Mexico. Today we create the Quesadilla, a warm flour tortilla loaded GOOEY GOODNESS and your favorite pairings.

Ingredients

  • Flour Tortillas
  • Protein of choice
  • Shredded Monterrey Jack Cheese
  • Chopped White Onion
  • Chopped cilantro
  • Guacamole
  • Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Warm your flour tortilla on a hot skillet
  2. Place cheese on one side of the tortilla
  3. After 15secs fold the tortilla in half
  4. Continuously flip until golden brown
  5. Load with your favorite toppings and pairings
  6. ENJOY!
