Taco Tuesday

Sonoratown, Los Angeles.

Ingredients

  • Yellow Corn Tortillas
  • Protein of choice
  • Chopped white onion
  • Chopped cilantro
  • Salsa of choosing

Today we will be cooking a traditional Mexican dish that has grabbed the hearts....and tongues of people across the globe. Tacos symbolize a culmination of regional influences with a very much traditional approach. With a tortilla de maiz as the base, we load the tortilla with your protein of choice and topped with onions, cilantro and salsa.
