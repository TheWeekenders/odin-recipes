Nachos Recipe

The best DAMN NACHOS

A party staple. Super Bowl favorite. The BEST DAMN NACHOS!

Ingredients

  • Tortilla Chips
  • Nacho Cheese
  • Protein of choice
  • Guacamole
  • Sour Cream
  • Slice Jalapenos
  • Cilantro Garnish

Steps

  1. Melt nacho cheese
  2. Load tortilla chips on plate
  3. First layer is ALWAYS cheese!
  4. Add your toppings
  5. Garnish
  6. ENJOY!
